Samak Meshwi

Prep: 15min | Marinate: 1hr | Cook: 20min | Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 1kg whole fish (hamour/kingfish)
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • Juice of 2 lemons
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Scale, gut, and clean fish. Make diagonal cuts on both sides.
  2. Mix all spices, garlic, lemon juice, oil, salt, pepper.
  3. Rub marinade inside and outside fish. Stuff with cilantro.
  4. Marinate 1 hour in fridge.
  5. Preheat grill to medium-high. Oil grates well.
  6. Grill 8-10 min per side until charred and flaky.
  7. Serve with lemon wedges and tahini sauce.
Per serving: 320kcal | 18g fat | 2g carbs | 38g protein
